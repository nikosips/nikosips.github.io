About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate and a researcher at the Visual Recognition Group, Czech Technical University in Prague. My supervisor is Professor Ondrej Chum.

My research lies in the intersection of computer vision and deep learning, and more specifically I focus on image representation learning for fine-grained image recognition tasks. I am mostly interested in developing large-scale image retrieval systems that work on the finest of granularities, down to the instance-level, and across multiple visual domains.

Prior to my Ph.D., I obtained my Engineering Diploma (5 year degree) at the school of Electrical & Computer Engineering, National Technical University of Athens.
